# 🔨 RojiRojgar

**RojiRojgar** (रोजी रोजगार) is a platform connecting **daily wage workers** with employers — making it fast, simple, and accessible for blue-collar workers to find work and for employers to hire trusted local labour.

> *"Rozi"* = daily livelihood | *"Rojgar"* = employment

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Millions of daily wage workers — carpenters, plumbers, electricians, painters, helpers, and more — struggle to find consistent work. Employers equally struggle to find verified, reliable workers quickly. RojiRojgar bridges this gap with a simple, mobile-first platform.

---

## ✨ Features

### For Workers
- 📋 Create a profile with skills, location & availability
- 🔔 Get notified of nearby job postings instantly
- 📍 Find jobs within your preferred radius
- ⭐ Build reputation through ratings & reviews
- 💰 Track earnings and work history

### For Employers
- 📣 Post jobs in under 2 minutes
- 🔍 Search verified workers by skill, location & availability
- ✅ View ratings, reviews & past work history
- 💬 Chat directly with workers
- 📆 Schedule and manage bookings
